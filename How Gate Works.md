How the hell does Gate work?  
Gate works on a very simple premise. Whenever you talk in a server's public channel, you gain one point, and are put on a cooldown before you can gain another point. As your point total rises, and as you pass thresholds set by the server's moderators, your trust level, and your assosicated discord roles, will change. While this is simmilar to other bots on discords that track user XP and levels, Gate is intended soley as a moderation tool. There is no way to view your own points, or other users points. Gate hands out roles whenever it needs to, and thats it.

What do I need to do, as a server memeber?  
Nothing. Gate works in the background, and will only act when your roles need to change. If your roles ever seem to get out of sync, you can ask a moderator to check and manually update your point total.

What do I need to do as a server moderator?  
Setting up gate can be a little involved. Reffer to the page on setup for more information.
